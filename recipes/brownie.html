Brownie Recipe

The best fudgy brownie recipe

Learn how to make your brownie with a fudgy center!

Ingredients

  • flour
  • sugar
  • cocoa powder
  • butter
  • egg

Steps

  1. Preheat oven.
  2. Combine butter, egg and sugar. Whisk well.
  3. Sift in flour and cocoa powder.
  4. Bake for 20 minutes.
